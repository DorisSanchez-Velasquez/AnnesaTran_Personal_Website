Improving Public Recycling

Description

When throwing out their waste, people often have to face the conflicitng question of whether an item belongs in the recycling bin or the trash. This had led to greater damage in recycling equipment and a increasingly harmful impact on the environment. the goal for this project was to create a tool to help user to parse out where each everyday item belongs in the waste.

Role

Annesa Tran specializes in user-interface design and prototyping. However, this project provided the perfect opportunity to branch out into new topics and gain expeirence in on user research design and qualitative analysis.

Prototype

Design solution was to have public trash cans integrated with an image-recognition system. When unsure of the type of disposal, the individual simply scans the item under a digital screen and the screen indicates whether the item is recycable or should be disposed in the trash.
